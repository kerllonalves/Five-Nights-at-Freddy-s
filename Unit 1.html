Five Nights at Freddy's

What is FNAF?

Five Nights at Freddy's (FNAF), is an indie horror video game franchise created by Scott Cawthon. First released on August 8, 2014.

It is about a pizzeria called Freddy Fazbear's Pizza with animal-like animatronics made for entertainment for children.

During the nightshift, you play as a security guard, you are put in a office and your job to watch over the pizzeria through security cameras. But the animatronics are haunted by the souls of children that were killed in the 1980's by a serial killer called William Afton. Now you need to survive the attacks of the animatronics by avoiding their entrance in your office til 6 am.

Today, FNAF is famous worldwide, becoming a multimedia huge franchise with not only being games anymore. With billions and billions of views on YouTube being a huge classic in the horror video game genre.

Being a huge multimedia franchise, today FNAF Contains:

  • More than 10 mainline games
  • Big collections of Books
  • Video game spinoffs
  • Huge collection of merchandise
  • A Full length movie

FNAF has a huge base of people that does fangames. Which fans uses the fnaf IP to create their own FNAF game with unique stories, characters, etc. Some of these games was so well made that the creator himself called these developers to turn these games into official spinoffs.

Even to this day, the franchise still going strong, with a lot more games and books releasing, a movie sequel coming, and it still relevant this day as the franchise keeps getting bigger and bigger.
